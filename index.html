About LadyBlzn

LadyBlzn creates raw alternative rap and grunge-inspired music about pain, survival, love, trauma, insecurity, and rising from the ashes.

The sound is dark, honest, emotional, rebellious, and built for anyone who has ever felt broken but refused to stay down.
